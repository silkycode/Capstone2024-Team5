Medical Forms

The medical forms needed depends on the medical supply you need.

Diabetic products and supplies:
Medicaid/Commerical CMN
Medicare CMN

Continuous Glucose Monitoring:
Certificate of Medical Necessity
Office notes that include notes from an in-person appointment within the past 6 months and A1c lab results
AL Medicaid Referral signed by EPSDT certified screener (Form 362)
Billing invoice for desired CGM Sample

Insulin Pumps:
Certificate of Medical Necessity
Office notes that include notes from an in-person appointment within the past 6 months and A1c lab results
Insulin pump training contract - proof that patient and guardian attended a training class on their selected insulin pump and will comply with doctor's orders.
6 weeks of blood glucose logs with at least 4 readings per day. Can be handwritten or pulled from CGM (Dexcom or Libre) database.
AL Medicaid Referral signed by EPSDT certified screener (Form 362)
Billing invoice for desired insulin pump
